Korean Sheet Masks: how to use them

Sheet masks are pre-soaked in serums and essences that are filled with the goodness of Collagen, Vitamin C, and Antioxidants. They nourish your skin perfectly and ensure that it’s glowing every day.

keep your face wet for few minutes after peeling the mask.

Sheet masks are mostly made up of eight different types of materials that retain serums and feel comfortable while using. Cotton: Cotton sheet masks are light in weight, white in colour, and feel smooth while applying. They have the highest soaking tendency which makes them rich in serum. Microfiber: The sheet masks made up of micro-fibre are slightly thicker than the cotton ones. They feel like a smooth silk cloth and are completely environmental-friendly. Cupra: They are the more transparent version of cotton sheet masks. Made up of cotton bi-product, they feel light on your skin and provide you with the most rejuvenating experience. Hydrogel: If you love peel-off masks, hydrogel face masks are the ones you should try for. However, you should be careful while handling them.
