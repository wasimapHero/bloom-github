5 Home Remedies for Frizzy Hair, Plus Tips for Prevention

Apple cider vinegar Coconut oil Argan oil Avocado Egg Products to try Tips for prevention Takeaway

Frizzy hair may be hard to tame, but it’s not impossible.

Frizziness is caused by dry hair that lacks moisture. Ironically, humid, wet weather tends to make frizzy hair worse. That’s because dry hair tries to absorb moisture out of the air, causing each hair’s cuticle, or outer layer, to swell up, instead of lying flat. The cuticle comprises overlapping scales, which separate and rise in moist air. This makes hair look frizzy. Anything that dries out hair can make frizz worse. This includes shampoos that are alkaline, and products, such as styling gels, that contain alcohol. Styling tools that use heat can also dry out hair, making frizz erupt. If you want to achieve a smoother look to your locks, there are home remedies that can restore moisture to help reduce frizz. The added benefit is that increased moisture can also help improve hair health.
